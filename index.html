01The Story

Every producer knows the feeling — the song is in your head, the recording is there, and then the mix becomes a wall.

Too many plugins. Too many options. Too little understanding of why one move works and another doesn't. So you stack tutorials, presets, third-party recommendations — and the craft slips further from you.

We started Pad Works because mixing shouldn't require a decade of trial and error. Technology should make the craft more accessible — not replace the human behind it. AI doesn't replace the engineer. It documents the decisions an engineer would make, and gives you back the time to make better ones.

So we built an ecosystem. Not just plugins — a system that analyzes, processes, and explains. Every knob has a reason. Every decision is documented. Every producer, from bedroom to studio, gets the same quality tools and the knowledge to use them.

Pad Works is the product brand of XPN, the professional audio division of TMCC — The Martin Creative Company. We design tools, write the software, and document the craft.
