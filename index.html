01 · Pads

Smart Audio Classifier

Drop 400 samples into a folder. XPN analyzes each sample's frequency spectrum — detecting kicks, snares, hats, and melodics by how they sound, not by filename. Auto-builds balanced kits in seconds.

02 · Sequencer

Bake patterns into the pack

Preview groove, pocket and swing inside XPN. Export the sequence with the pack so buyers load a kit and hear your demo immediately.

How it works

From folder to finished pack
in under ten minutes.

4 steps

Drop. Assign. Tune. Ship.

XPN Builder is the only tool in the market that treats your expansion pack like a studio session — because that's what it is. Built by an indie dev in Santiago who was tired of XML surgery and Expansion Builder crashes.

  • ① Drop a folder of samples — 1 to 2,000.
  • ② Pick a genre template. Auto-assign runs.
  • ③ Edit pads, trim samples, bake a demo pattern.
  • ④ Export to every target — MPC 2, MPC 3, Ableton, MIDI.
