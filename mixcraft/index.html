01What it does

Not a magic button. A mix engineer.

Mixcraft doesn't replace your taste. It documents the decisions a great engineer would make for your session — and tells you why. You stay in control. You learn as you go.

24×

Track Classification

24-type taxonomy powered by Claude Haiku. Kick, snare, lead vocal, harmony, sub-bass, ambient pad — Mixcraft knows what's in your session.

M×N

Masking Matrix

Pairwise conflict detection across every track combination. Spectral overlap, transient collisions, dynamic range fights — visualized in one matrix.

RX

Recipe Generation

Mixing recipes adapted to your plugin kit. Mixcraft inventories what you own — BALANZE, FX Suite, third-party — and writes moves you can actually execute.

EDU

Educational by Design

Every decision is explained. Why this cut, why this compression ratio, why this side-chain. You're not just running a tool — you're learning the craft.

WS

WebSocket Push to BALANZE

One click pushes the recipe into BALANZE Canal and Master across every channel of your session. Live, on localhost:9090, no upload required.

LCL

Local-First, Private

Audio analysis runs on your machine. Only structured metadata (LUFS, spectral peaks, track types) talks to the cloud. Your stems never leave.
